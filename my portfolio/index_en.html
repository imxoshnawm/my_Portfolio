3. Conceptualized Projects

Glove Talk

A speech-enabled glove designed to address communication challenges faced by the hearing impaired.

This is my favorite conceptual project that I have envisioned for over two years. Due to development challenges and lack of support, it has not been implemented yet.

I conceived this idea because, as a 24-year-old with hearing impairment, I have struggled to fully integrate with society beyond my family.

Recent statistics indicate that approximately 250,000 people in Kurdistan and Iraq suffer from hearing and speech difficulties—with nearly 40% being school-age children or young adults, and over 70% facing employment or educational barriers due to communication issues.

Glove Talk aims to convert sign language into speech and text, enabling direct communication with anyone.
